overview

The 4-day tour in three countries offering you the experience of Myanmar's most historic sites and culture. Exploring the ancient Bagan with thousands of temples and pagodas. Get the best views of Bagan’s 8,000 temples and pagodas, all of which are nigh on impossible to visit on land, during an extra-special hot-air balloon flight. Taking a morning flight means you’ll watch the sunrise and see the area at its most magical, when morning mist shrouds the greenery. This tour takes you deep into our beautiful country, immersing you in our rich culture and elegant landscapes. The entire tour is accompanied by experienced, local, English speaking guides.
